Our Manufacturing Process

From raw materials to finished products - discover our precision manufacturing process

Precision Manufacturing Excellence

At Sina Steel, we combine traditional craftsmanship with modern technology to produce the highest quality stainless steel products. Our manufacturing process is designed to ensure consistency, precision, and excellence in every product we create.

Our Manufacturing Steps

1

Material Selection & Inspection

We carefully select premium-grade stainless steel materials from trusted suppliers. Each batch undergoes rigorous quality inspection to ensure it meets our strict standards for composition, purity, and mechanical properties.

  • Chemical composition analysis
  • Mechanical property testing
  • Surface quality inspection
  • Traceability documentation
2

Precision Cutting & Shaping

Using state-of-the-art cutting equipment, we precisely cut and shape the stainless steel according to customer specifications. Our advanced machinery ensures accuracy within 0.1mm tolerance for all dimensions.

  • CNC precision cutting
  • Laser cutting technology
  • Water jet cutting
  • Custom dimensioning
3

Forming & Bending

Our skilled craftsmen use specialized forming equipment to bend and shape the stainless steel into the required configurations. This process maintains the material's structural integrity while achieving the desired geometry.

  • Press brake forming
  • Roll forming
  • Tube bending
  • Complex geometry forming
4

Welding & Assembly

Our certified welders use advanced welding techniques including TIG, MIG, and orbital welding to join components with precision. All welds are inspected and tested to ensure they meet the highest quality standards.

  • TIG welding
  • MIG welding
  • Orbital welding
  • Weld inspection
5

Surface Treatment

We apply various surface treatments to enhance the appearance, durability, and performance of our stainless steel products. This includes polishing, passivation, and specialized coatings as required.

  • Mechanical polishing
  • Electropolishing
  • Passivation treatment
  • Specialized coatings
6

Quality Control & Testing

Every product undergoes comprehensive quality control testing including dimensional checks, surface finish inspection, and mechanical property verification. We maintain detailed records of all testing procedures and results.

  • Dimensional inspection
  • Surface finish testing
  • Mechanical testing
  • Documentation
