Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

Are the products Food Grade?

Yes, all our stainless steel products in grades 304 and 316 are Food Grade compliant. These grades are specifically designed for food and pharmaceutical applications, ensuring they meet the highest standards for hygiene and safety. Our products are certified to international standards including ASTM, DIN, and EN.

Absolutely! We understand that different projects have different requirements. We can supply both small quantities for individual projects and large quantities for industrial applications. Our flexible supply chain allows us to accommodate orders ranging from a few pieces to several tons. Contact us with your specific requirements, and we'll provide a customized solution.
