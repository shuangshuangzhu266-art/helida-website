About Sina Steel

Excellence in stainless steel solutions for over 5 years

0
+ Years of Excellence

With over 5 years of experience in the Egyptian market, Sina Steel has become a trusted supplier in the stainless steel sector, offering integrated supply solutions for the food, pharmaceutical, and various industrial sectors.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of premium stainless steel solutions in Egypt and the Middle East, recognized for our quality, reliability, and innovation in serving diverse industrial sectors.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional stainless steel products and services that meet international standards, while building lasting partnerships with our clients through superior quality, competitive pricing, and reliable supply chain management.

Our Core Values

Quality Excellence

We maintain the highest standards in all our products and services, ensuring compliance with international standards ASTM, DIN, and EN.

Customer Trust

Building long-term relationships through reliable service, transparent communication, and consistent delivery of promises.

Innovation

Continuously improving our processes and exploring new technologies to better serve our clients' evolving needs.

Reliability

Consistent delivery, on-time service, and dependable supply chain management that our clients can count on.
