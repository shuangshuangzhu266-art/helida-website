Our Quality Commitment

At Sina Steel, we are committed to ensuring that all stainless steel products we supply are compliant with international standards ASTM / DIN / EN. We guarantee that the materials used are Food Grade (304 / 316) and free from any manufacturing defects. We also pledge to replace any supplies proven to be non-compliant with the agreed-upon specifications, ensuring our customers' peace of mind.

Food Grade Materials

All our stainless steel products in grades 304 and 316 are specifically designed for food and pharmaceutical applications. These materials are:

  • Corrosion resistant and non-reactive
  • Easy to clean and maintain
  • Hygienic and safe for food contact
  • Durable and long-lasting
